Property details

Listing # WL26115 | Listed on 9 August

AUCTION: Friday 1st September 2023 at 12:00pm, Harcourts Wellington City Office (unless sold prior)

This delightful 3-bedroom, two-bathroom home offers comfort, convenience, and charm, combined with great parking and fabulous sun.

Nestled in the serene Paparangi hills, this picturesque living environment provides stunning vistas of Johnsonville and the surrounding bush clad hills. Relax on the patio deck and savour enchanting sunsets and the ever-changing vistas.

The main bedroom and ensuite bathroom are situated on the ground floor, ensuring convenience, privacy and easy access. Upstairs, you'll find two spacious bedrooms, an additional family bathroom, offering comfort and amenities for all.

Also upstairs, the inviting open plan kitchen, living, and dining areas flow effortlessly, providing a generous space for the whole family to enjoy together. These areas open to the west facing deck and east facing back yard. The views are exceptional as you bathe in the late sun.

A heat pump and DVS heat transfer system ensure that warmth is evenly distributed throughout the home, maintaining a comfortable and pleasant atmosphere.

The easily accessed well-manicured outdoor area is perfect for peaceful relaxation without the burden of extensive upkeep. Keep it as is, or transformed it into a beautiful vegetable garden, this space can become your private oasis.

Set back from the road, this home offers a peaceful and tranquil environment, free from street noise complete with two off-street carparks and internal access garaging.

Don't miss the opportunity to own this cozy and inviting property in Paparangi. Make 29a Pelorous Street your new address today!

Contact us today to arrange a viewing and discover the comfort and charm this property has to offer.
