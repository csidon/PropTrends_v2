Property details

Listing # L23414775 | Listed on 27 July

This is a fantastic opportunity to own a remarkable apartment in the heart of Thorndon. This two-bedroom spacious gem offers 74sqm of modern living space with many desirable features.

Two spacious double bedrooms with wardrobes

High stud height throughout not only adds to the overall spaciousness but also gives a sense of grandeur to the living spaces

Enjoy the luxury of a generously sized tiled bedroom plus a second toilet for added convenience

The open plan layout seamlessly connects the living room and kitchen, creating an inviting and inclusive environment

Breath-taking views stretching from Thorndon to the magnificent Wellington harbour, providing a captivating backdrop day and night

A separate laundry room and ample storage throughout

The NBS rating is 75%

Whiteware and furniture included which is perfect for investment buyers or someone wanting a city pad during the working week

The postal address of this apartment at 'Thorndon Rise' is Unit 2 and it is located on level 3

  • BC fees $15,390.48

Don't miss out on this opportunity to own a spacious two-bedroom apartment with stunning views in the heart of Wellington close to all amenities and Parliament buildings. Whether you're looking for a great place to call home or a sound investment, this apartment ticks all the right boxes. A must-see for anyone seeking comfort, style, and convenience and is centrally located so you can enjoy Wellington's vibrant lifestyle.

Contact Joanna or Alice to arrange a time to view.

Tender closes 2pm Thursday 17th August at Tommys Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Wellington (the vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
