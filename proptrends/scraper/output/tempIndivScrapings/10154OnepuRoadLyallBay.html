Property details

Listing # L23617092 | Listed on 1 August

Located within a minutes' walk to Lyall Bay beach plus great cafes, bars and restaurants this sunny ground floor unit provides easy care living within a friendly community. Its features include:

Open plan living/dining with potential to close off dining to provide a second bedroom

Double bedroom with great wardrobe space

Functional kitchen with good storage

Bathroom including bath and separate toilet

Sunny courtyard/patio area with lawn, perfect for vegetable planters

Off street dedicated car park

Mostly double glazed with heatpump

Crosslease title therefore no Body Corporate fee only $600 per annum for grounds maintenance

NBS rating 67%

A perfect opportunity to jump on the property ladder, increase your investment portfolio or have a sunny bolt hole in a fantastic location.

Tender closes 2pm 22nd August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
