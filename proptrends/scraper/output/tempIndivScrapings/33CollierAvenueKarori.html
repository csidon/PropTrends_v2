Property details

Listing # L23346323 | Listed on 31 July

Welcome to 33 Collier Avenue Karori, a super sunny family home. This property boasts four double bedrooms and two bathrooms, providing ample space for a growing family. With a double garage and open parking spaces, there is plenty of room for vehicles.

The home sits on a generous 487 sqm of land, offering a spacious backyard for outdoor activities. The building area spans 240 sqm, providing plenty of room for comfortable living. Constructed in 1960, this is the first time this home has ever come to the public market.

Further features include -

• Open planning kitchen and dining area

• Modern and spacious kitchen with generous storage

• Separate family living room with open fire and deck access

• Four double bedrooms with built in wardrobes

• Family sized bathroom with separate shower and bath

• Large guest bedroom or second lounge area

• Three sunny decks - perfect for outdoor entertaining or simply enjoying the sunshine

• Heating options include, open fire, heat pump, panel heaters and underfloor heating

• Well looked after and cared for

• High stud and native timber flooring throughout

• Large double garage with workshop - plus two off street carparks

• Flat front yard area

• Zoned for Wellington girls, Wellington College

Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to own a lovely family home. Enquire now to secure your viewing of 33 Collier Avenue Karori.

LIM and builders report available upon viewing this home - To view, please phone Ramon Kane 021 377 172 or ramon@tommys.co.nz

Tender closes 2pm 23th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington ( Vendor reserves the right to sell prior ).
