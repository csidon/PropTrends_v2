Property details

Listing # L23295152 | Listed on 27 July

The perfect urban retreat in a highly sought-after city-fringe location.

This well-presented, sunny, north-west facing Thorndon apartment in the popular Stadium Gardens offers the ideal combination of convenience and calmness.

Highlights include:

  • Two double bedrooms; the primary bedroom offers a large walk-in robe and a delightful outlook
  • Spacious modern open-plan kitchen, dining and living area
  • Flow from the living space to a north-west facing balcony, the perfect spot to enjoy the sun and pleasant views across Thorndon and beyond to the lush greenbelt
  • Ample room in the living space to accommodate a workstation to work from home
  • Sizeable bathroom cleverly incorporating a laundry area for added convenience
  • A secure car park and storage locker provide the ultimate peace of mind
  • New curtains, blinds, and carpet throughout
  • Current Body Corporate Levy: $15,825.60pa
  • Currently tenanted with a periodic tenancy in place
  • IEP EQ Rating of 62% New Building Standard
This apartment complex has something truly special. A fabulous open atrium sits at the centre of the building, flooding the interior with natural light. Planters line the atrium, creating a soothing green edge that compliments the spaciousness of the complex.

Appealing to busy professionals, a small family or those looking to downsize to apartment living. An easy walk to the CBD, Railway Station, Supermarket and Sky Stadium.

Tender (If Not Sold Prior), Closing 2.00pm, Wednesday 16 August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington

An information pack, including Body Corporate information, will be made available to parties who have viewed the property and registered their interest.
