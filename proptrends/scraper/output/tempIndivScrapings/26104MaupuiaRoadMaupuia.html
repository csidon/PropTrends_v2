Property details

Listing # L22666883 | Listed on 8 August

Nestled in a sheltered position this exquisite three double bedroom, two bathroom home enjoys all day sun with a clever indoor-outdoor flow.

Built to a very high standard in 2019 to this executive home offers the perfect blend of modern elegance, multiple entertaining areas, and comfortable living. Further features include :

  • Designer galley kitchen offering functionality equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and premium cabinetry
  • Open plan living/dining with the ability to create multiple living areas
  • Large double garage with internal access and additional storage space
  • Multiple decks and outdoor areas to maximise the orientation to all day sun
  • Three double bedrooms. Master bedroom complete with an ensuite and spacious walk-in wardrobe
  • Tiled family bathroom that includes separate bath, designed for both functionality and style plus additional guest toilet
  • Double glazed and fully insulated
  • Fantastic sun streams into the home throughout with great indoor/outdoor flow to private decks.
  • Easy access to local amenities, schools, parks, and transportation plus a short drive to Miramar Village

This home must be seen to be believed. Don't miss out on the opportunity to call this remarkable property your home, experience the perfect balance of contemporary luxury and functional design in the heart of Maupuia.

Tender closes 12pm 30th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
