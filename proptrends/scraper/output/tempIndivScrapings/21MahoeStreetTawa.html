Property details

Listing # PE9882 | Listed on 24 July

Introducing a stunning home that has been beautifully renovated whilst still retaining the charm of its 1950s origins.

This modernised residence enjoys a designer kitchen ideal for culinary enthusiasts, spacious living & dining and a separate study for those who require a dedicated workspace.

Attracting ample natural light, it offers a warm and inviting atmosphere throughout.

Located close to schools and public transport, fully fenced and with an outdoor space perfect for relaxing or entertaining, this property ensures your privacy and security.

Don't miss the opportunity to make this your dream home - Contact Team Ritchie for your private viewing or we look forward to seeing you at the open home.
