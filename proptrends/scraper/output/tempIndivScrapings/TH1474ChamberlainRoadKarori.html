Property details

Listing # WTN10489 | Listed on 7 August

Stage one has sold out, final release available now, and move in ready.

Designed for premium modern urban lifestyle Riviera Group bring you Norfolk Terraces a bespoke new housing development on Chamberlain Road. Designed by Sheppard & Rout Architects and in the desirable established Wellington suburb of Karori.

Norfolk Terraces celebrates materials of exceptional quality and sophistication, on offer are terraced Freehold Town houses that embrace their native bush surroundings. Two and three bedroom homes come with their own carpark and beautiful outdoor living spaces.

  • EV car charging
  • Onsite car parking per dwelling
  • Sunny aspect and lush green outlook
  • Premium kitchen with Stone benchtops
  • Laneway areas
  • Heat Pumps
  • Bike parking
  • Lockable storage lockers all dwellings
  • Hideaway laundry
  • Coat and shoe cupboard/under stair storage
  • Just 800m to local mall and public transport

Enjoy low maintenance easy living close to an abundance of local amenities including an array of shops and Cafes, Karori Mall, New World, Countdown, Library, Petrol stations and a range of schools: Karori Normal School, Wellington High school, Wellington Girls' College and Wellington College.

Lifestyle, liveability, quality, and affordability are the focus here. There is only a very limited number available; you must act quickly to secure yours today.

https://www.propertyfiles.co.nz/property/NORFOLK

Price guide from $795,000.
