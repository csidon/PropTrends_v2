Property details

Listing # 3326127 | Listed on 25 July

Discover your dream home nestled on Belmont's Western Hills.

This spacious three-bedroom property offers a comfortable and modern living experience, perfect for families and couples alike.

Features include:

  • An open plan layout seamlessly connects the kitchen, dining area, and lounge, creating a spacious and inviting atmosphere for entertaining guests and spending quality family time.

  • The modern kitchen is well equipped and also provides a servery allowing for easy severing and socialising or a place for kid's breakfasts and homework.

  • The spacious bathroom has also been upgraded and provides shower, toilet vanity and a separate bath.

  • Stay cozy during winter months with a log burner and a heat transfer system that efficiently distributes warmth throughout the home. For warmer days, a heat pump ensures optimal comfort, while underfloor insulation helps maintain a pleasant temperature year-round.

  • Separate office; ideal for remote work or study.

  • A single garage and carport and the convenience of off-street parking provides ample space for your vehicles and storage needs.

Perfectly positioned, this home is in close proximity to reputable schools such as Raphael House Rudolf Steiner School, Maranatha School and Belmont Primary school.

Lower Hutt's CBD is just a short drive and provides access to a wide range of amenities, including shopping, dining and entertainment options. A bus stop is close by and ensures easy commuting and connectivity to Queensgate and Melling railway station.

Don't miss the opportunity to make this beautiful Belmont property your new home.

To be sold by Tender Thursday 10 August 2023 (unless sold prior), 2pm at our Lower Hutt Office - 120 Queens Drive, Lower Hutt.
