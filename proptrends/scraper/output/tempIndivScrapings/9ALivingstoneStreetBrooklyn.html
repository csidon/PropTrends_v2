Property details

Listing # L23539186 | Listed on 3 August

This architect-designed marvel boasts a remarkable Homestar 7 Built Rating, a testament to its top-notch performance in energy efficiency and sustainability. Step into the epitome of modern living!

  • Embrace cost-effective living with energy efficiency, keeping your power bills low, while better for the environment and a healthier you.
  • Stay perfectly comfortable year-round with the thoughtfully crafted building envelope, built with 150mm thick walls, above code insulation. High-performance, thermally broken windows ensure abundant sunlight and breathtaking views.
  • Experience fresh, healthy air with the German tech integration, featuring a Lunos ERV system that keeps the air and your living space fresh and dry. Perfect for managing asthma and hay fever.
  • 4 Heat Pumps (mainly for cooling) Wi-Fi controlled
  • Acoustically insulated internal walls and careful placement of wardrobes between bedrooms mean exceptional acoustic performance.
  • Designer LED lighting, tastefully adorning every corner of this remarkable residence, creating the perfect ambience.
  • 2 off-street car parks, ensuring convenient parking.
  • A high-end kitchen featuring Blum hardware, Corian, and stainless steel benchtops.
  • Abundant storage solutions include built-in wardrobes and ample space throughout the house.

Built just 1.5 years ago, this home flawlessly merges contemporary design with functionality. Experience the warmth and natural light throughout the day on the all-day sunlit NW-facing deck and lawn. The low-maintenance native plantings ensure effortless landscaping, letting you savour the natural beauty with ease.

Don't miss this rare opportunity to secure your slice of paradise! Contact us now to schedule a viewing and make this yours. Welcome home! Tender closes 2pm 17th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
