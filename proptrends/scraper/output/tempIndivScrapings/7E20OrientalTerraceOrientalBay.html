Property details

Listing # WL26122 | Listed on 10 August

TENDER CLOSES: Wednesday 30th August 2023 at 2:00pm, Harcourts Wellington City Office (unless sold prior)

Located in the well-regarded Jerningham complex, this two bedroom apartment is an ideal home for downsizers, professionals, commuters or those who love beach-side living. This seventh floor apartment comprises an open plan living, kitchen and dining area that is both light and spacious with a generous slice of a sparkling Oriental Bay view. The bathroom has been recently remodelled, and there's brand new carpet throughout. Both bedrooms are doubles - the second bedroom with clever internal and external windows to make the most of the light and sun. Ample storage can be found throughout including a superb walk-in utility cupboard, large wardrobes in both bedrooms and ample kitchen cabinetry.

All the whiteware is included, and the furniture and fittings are open for negotiation - right down to the teaspoons - so you can take it over fully furnished and be in straight away if desired. A single garage (with electronic door) is a sought-after bonus in this exclusive location.

As a Company-Share property, Jerningham is largely owner-occupied which creates a sense of safety, stability and community. The secure front door system with electronic entrance, dual lifts plus an attentive building manager ensures convenience as well as peace of mind. A spectacular feature is the shared rooftop terrace with its kitchen, BBQ, tables, bathrooms and expansive harbour views to enjoy with friends and family.

Quarterly levies include council rates, maintenance and building insurance are a very reasonable $2,307.20. WCC earthquake rating is 84%.

These apartments come up rarely so don't hesitate - our owners are firmly looking to move on after 7 years of happy living. See you at the open homes or call for your private viewing.
