Property details

Listing # 3305873 | Listed on 1 August

Welcome to your perfect oasis for family living! This fantastic 4 bedroom, 2 bathroom home is a true gem nestled in the heart of Kilbirnie. Boasting fantastic views, this property offers a blend of comfort and convenience.

Key Features:

4 Spacious Bedrooms: With 3 double bedrooms upstairs and a spacious double bedroom downstairs which includes its own bathroom. A layout that is perfect for kids going to university, an elderly family member or you could even explore the possibility of renting it

out.

Fantastic Views: Perched up on Childers Terrace, The living space soaks in all day sunshine whilst looking out toward the Eastern suburbs, Evans and Shelly Bay.

Prime Location: Nestled in a family-friendly community, this home is close to reputable schools, parks, and all essential amenities. Commuting is a breeze with easy access to public transport

Off Street car park with Garage: Keep your vehicle safe and sound with a garage and an OSP on the driveway space in front of it.

Community Pool Nearby: A 5 minute walk to WRAC (Wellington regional aquatic centre) to enjoy the perks of a community pool during warm summer days, or sauna and spa on those cold winter nights.

Don't miss this golden opportunity to make this remarkable family home yours! Embrace a lifestyle of comfort, convenience, and stunning views. Contact us today to schedule your own private inspection
