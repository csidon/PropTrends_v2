Property details

Listing # L23424988 | Listed on 26 July

Enviably situated in the charming and popular Futuna Close this impeccably presented townhouse is situated in the heart of Karori and located within an easy walk to the Karori shopping centre, schools, buses and all the wonderful local amenities. this property is perfectly situated for modern convenience.

Further features include:

  • Freehold titles - no body corporate
  • Super sunny west facing aspect
  • Generous light filled open plan living and kitchen space with flow to a fabulous private deck - perfect for entertaining your guests
  • Convenience of garaging with internal access
  • Zoned for Wellington College, Wellington Girls College and Karori Normal School
  • Enjoy the beautiful historic chapel set in the grounds of the complex

Futuna Close is a beautiful quiet and well maintained complex with friendly neighbours and set amongst birdsong in a bush-clad setting. This fabulous home offers a sense of security, privacy and is a wonderful lifestyle opportunity.

Tender closes 2pm 16th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
