Property details

Listing # WL26113 | Listed on 9 August

AUCTION: Friday 1st September 2023 at 12:00pm, Harcourts Wellington City Office (unless sold prior)

Nestled in the heart of Johnsonville, this delightful 3-bedroom + office, 2-bathroom property offers the ideal blend of comfort and convenience.

Situated a quick drive away from shops and public transport, this home provides the best of both worlds - a peaceful retreat without sacrificing proximity to essential amenities. Schools are conveniently located nearby, ensuring a seamless daily routine for you and your family.

Clad with 100% recyclable low maintenance Palliside weatherboards, this property is easy care and boasts a large double car garage with internal access, offering plenty of room for your vehicles, a laundry, and additional storage.

Step inside to discover a world of modern living and thoughtful design. The upstairs level features a spacious double bedroom and bathroom giving the user total peace and quiet from the rest of the home. Also on this level is a dedicated study / office, perfect for working from home and giving you the flexibility to create your own space.

Downstairs you will find the heart of the home, the large west facing open-plan living areas and well-appointed kitchen that open to a wraparound deck. The perfect hub for relaxation and entertaining together with family or friends.

The clean and well-appointed kitchen with its breakfast bar bench adds a touch of convenience, making meal preparation whilst enjoying family time an absolute breeze.

Two spacious double bedrooms with built in wardrobes, and the second bathroom, complete the lower level.

The expansive decking/balcony that spans the entire length of the house offers the perfect spot to soak in the sun and enjoy the outdoors and the urban and bush clad views. Embrace the potential to transform this area into your own outdoor oasis, tailor-made to suit your lifestyle.

Secluded at the end of a private shared driveway, 84b Woodland Road offers the security and tranquillity you desire, ensuring a sense of privacy in your new abode.

Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to own a property that perfectly balances comfort and convenience.

Contact us today to arrange a viewing of 84b Woodland Road, Johnsonville. Embrace a lifestyle of blissful living in this wonderful, low maintenance home!
