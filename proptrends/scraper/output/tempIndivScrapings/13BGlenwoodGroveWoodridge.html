Property details

Listing # L1156601 | Listed on 4 August

Welcome to this charming and inviting family home - a perfect choice for both first-time homebuyers and investors alike. The home offers practicality and convenience with a garage and generous storage areas on the lower level, leading to a hallway and a bedroom. As you ascend the stairs, you'll discover two more spacious bedrooms, both of which are doubles, a well-appointed bathroom, a laundry room, and a delightful open-plan living/dining area that seamlessly connects to the kitchen. The large windows in this space allow natural light to flood in, creating a warm and inviting ambiance.

The rear of the home boasts a sunny, north-facing, and private deck - an ideal spot for hosting summer BBQs with friends and family. The tranquil setting makes it the perfect retreat to relax and unwind.

Located in the peaceful Glenwood Grove, this neighborhood is not a 'through-road,' ensuring a safe and serene environment for families and pets. With easy access to Woodridge Drive, transportation is a breeze, and you'll find yourself conveniently close to local schools, shops, and other amenities.

The house exudes light and a welcoming atmosphere, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a cozy and comfortable home. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer looking to settle down or an investor seeking a profitable opportunity, this property is a must-see. Don't miss out - call us today or join one of our upcoming open homes to experience this fantastic opportunity for yourself!
