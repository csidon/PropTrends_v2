Property details

Listing # L23793146 | Listed on 7 August

Be the first to live in this stunning, brand new, corner apartment. Located on the 8th floor with a sunny West aspect, you'll enjoy coming home to a warm, light, and sunny space.

The Victoria at 120 Victoria Street is a superb location, built by GibbonsCo, one of Wellingtons most respected developers and renowned Wellington architects Designgroup Stapleton Elliott. The perfect space for professionals, students and downsizers. This will also appeal to first home buyers looking for a central city home, First Home Buyer grant may apply here too.

  • Double-glazed new windows
  • Acoustically rated inter-tenancy walls
  • Stylish kitchen with F & P appliances
  • Completion late 2023
  • Super sunny west facing aspect

This stylish block of apartments offers easy access to Wellington CBD, Vic University and a plethora of fine eateries. A short stroll to Wellingtons beautiful waterfront and Cuba Street. Combine all this with easy access to a wide range of amenities and you quickly see why 120 Victoria Street is great place to live!

With completion estimated late 2023 you can be living your best life in this modern brand-new apartment in no time.

Please note renders are indicative only and of the whole development, not just unit 808.
