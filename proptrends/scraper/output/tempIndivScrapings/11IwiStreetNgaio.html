Property details

Listing # KHD30196 | Listed on 18 July

There's much to love about 11 Iwi Street - single-level, solid and well maintained, weatherboard exterior and ColorSteel roof, two garages, a great location.. and pretty as a picture!

Extensively renovated in keeping with its 1940's heritage, the interior has retained much of its original charm and character to create a larger, tastefully decorated home with well-connected living spaces and a fabulous indoor-outdoor flow.

Entertaining and relaxing is so easy here whether indoors, or on the large sunny deck that works well as a safe play zone for children.

Also featuring three double bedrooms including the main with a sparkling ensuite plus family bathroom, and for all that Wellington's winter is able to conjure there's the choice of gas heating or a wood burner to keep toasty warm.

Two garages (the original single and much newer double) offer excellent parking and storage options, although with public transport literally a few minutes' walk away you might choose to leave the car at home.

Conveniently located for Ngaio School and Ngaio village.

Phone for more information, an appointment to view or join me at one of the open homes.
