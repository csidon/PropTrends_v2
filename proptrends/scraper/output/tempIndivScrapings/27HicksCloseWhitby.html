Property details

Listing # PE9896 | Listed on 7 August

Unleash your property dreams in the sought-after suburb of Whitby! Nestled away in quiet a cul-de-sac, this three-bedroom gem boasts a sun-kissed elevated position and ample parking. While some renovations have already been completed, there's still room for you to make your mark and create the home of your dreams."

Step inside to find a warm and welcoming atmosphere, enhanced by a cozy fireplace that invites you to snuggle up during winter evenings. Need a dedicated workspace? Look no further, as this home features a connected study conveniently adjoining the master bedroom.

Experience the beauty of seamless indoor-outdoor living with a layout designed for comfort and convenience. The fully fenced backyard ensures both privacy and security, allowing you to enjoy leisure time or entertain guests with ease. Plus, there's a delightful conservatory where you can bask in the sunlight and relish your own personal space.

Whether you are a first-time homebuyer or a savvy investor looking to expand your portfolio, this property is the perfect fit for your needs. For added peace of mind, we have comprehensive building and Lim reports available upon request.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to experience this fantastic property firsthand. Contact Martin Cardno on 0272865331 today to arrange a viewing and seize the chance to witness all that this home has to offer in person. Your dream home awaits!
