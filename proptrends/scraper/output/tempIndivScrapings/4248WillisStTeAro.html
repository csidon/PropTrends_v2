Property details

Listing # L23597744 | Listed on 8 August

BEO $445,000

SMART / CHARACTER / CENTRAL / REFURBISHED

LITTLE GEM. Very cute two (2) bedroom Wellington inner-city apartment at 248 Willis St in the Anderson Apartments. Incredibly central. Perfect proximity to all of Wellington's key attractions and the great array of funky eateries. All within a short stroll from home.

Quaint character building with loads of charm that you simply don't get with newer builds. These qualities are further accentuated by the high stud, steel frame windows and vintage wooden doors. And, not forgetting the balcony.

In excellent condition. Brand new refurbished kitchen and dining area. New carpet throughout. This is outstanding inner-city apartment living.

PROPERTY VALUES:

Good use of space - 70 sqm floor area. Impressive high stud. Two (2) bedroom. Ideal apartment perfect open plan kitchen / dining / living area (and balcony). Bus stop outside takes you into the CBD and Railways station. Reasonable Body Corp fees.
