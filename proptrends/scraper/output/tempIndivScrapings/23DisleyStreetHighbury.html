Property details

Listing # KOR30261 | Listed on 27 July

Presenting all the dreamy classic features of an early 1900's villa, this gorgeous home combines the integrity of character with the modern elements of contemporary, family living.

A casual walk up, arriving in an unexpected world of its own, the elevated, tranquil setting takes in picture-perfect views, tremendous bird life, and soaks up the sun to the very end of the day.

Be wowed by the high stud, spacious proportions and cohesive layout throughout.

There's a beautiful north-west facing living room with easy flow to a sophisticated light-filled chef's kitchen, and a stunning open-plan family living and dining area. Step outside to the adjoining courtyard, perfect for alfresco dining. There's flat lawn, established planting and complete privacy, as well as a cute separate studio/sleep out and a glasshouse. Four spacious double bedrooms, ensuite and a modern bathroom come complete with double glazed joinery, temper vent central system and full insulation.

The suburb of Highbury is Wellington's best kept secret, with a real sense of community and tranquillity, being surrounded by protected green space and bordering Zealandia. Kelburn Village is within walking distance and the bus stop is literally at the end of the path. You can easily walk to the CBD and even grab a coffee from Aro Street on your way. Zoned for Kelburn Normal School, as well as Wellington Girls and Wellington College, this home embraces the best of family living in one of Wellington's coolest spots.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Tenders close 1pm Thursday 10 August at our office at 136 Karori Road, Marsden Village, Karori.
