Property details

Listing # L23586898 | Listed on 1 August

Discover the perfect blend of comfort and convenience in this fabulous home, located in a prime Miramar location. Step inside and you'll be greeted by the warm and inviting ambiance that fills every corner. All on one level, the well-designed floor plan ensures a seamless flow throughout.

Special features include:

  • Two spacious bedrooms with built-in wardrobes
  • Central modern bathroom with underfloor heating
  • Delightful kitchen, dining, and generous lounge
  • High stud ceiling with a touch of original charm and character
  • Partially double glazed for extra warmth
  • Close to great schools, shops and transport
  • Beautifully landscaped garden complete with an irrigation system and flat lawn
  • Indoor/outdoor flow, perfect for entertaining
  • Great sun aspect
  • Drive-on tandem parking for at least two cars

The nature of this property makes it an ideal option for those seeking an easy-care lifestyle. Embrace the opportunity to make this home your own. Whether you're a first-time buyer searching for your ideal starter home, a family looking to downsize or an astute investor - this property ticks all the boxes. Don't miss out on the chance to secure this extremely desirable home in a great, family-friendly Wellington suburb.

Contact us now for your appointment to view.

Tender Closes 2pm Wednesday 16th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
