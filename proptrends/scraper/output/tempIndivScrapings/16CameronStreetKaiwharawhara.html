Property details

Listing # L22751502 | Listed on 27 July

With undoubtedly the best views of our stunning Wellington harbour on offer, 16 Cameron Street Kaiwharawhara will really impress. Just over 17 years young, the build style is contemporary and the presentation immaculate. Look at what this superb home offers:

  • Smart design over 2 levels with a generous 244sqm floor area and double internal access garage
  • Three double bedrooms, 2 with walk-in wardrobes and the 3rd with double built-in robe
  • Stylish tiled bathrooms plus additional guest toilet for total convenience
  • Open plan living with large windows (all double glazed) for maximum light
  • Flame gas fire and underfloor heating in tiled areas for year-round warmth and comfort
  • Decking on both levels for the best year-round indoor/outdoor living
  • Low maintenance property style where the emphasis is on quality, easy living

And the location is as good as it gets - right on the fringe of the city in an exclusive and tightly held residential area where you are just minutes from the city yet far enough away for peace and quiet. The great thing about this home is you can simply move in, put your feet up and enjoy the living experience and views this quality home has to offer - stunning by day, breath-taking by night. The complete Wellington residence.

Call today to view or see you at the open home!

Tender closes 3pm 16th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
