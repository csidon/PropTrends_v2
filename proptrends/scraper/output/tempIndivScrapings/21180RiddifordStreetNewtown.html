Property details

Listing # 4027230 | Listed on 1 December 2022

The modern architecturally-designed Masina Apartments were completed in 2013 and are centrally located on the corner of Riddiford and Hall Street in Newtown, Wellington.

Apartment 211 is a spacious one double bedroom apartment with an adjacent dual-key studio with separate entry. Designed by renowned Wellington Architects Novak and Middleton to offer a high standard of contemporary urban living for a discerning owner occupier as a house-and-income, or together as a dual-income investment property for the savvy investor.

The overall complex has stunning street appeal, modern safety and security systems and a comfortable level of privacy. Quality fittings and fixtures are used throughout. Generous living spaces, warmth and good natural light create a relaxed, urban atmosphere.

The area is rich in culture, cafes, shopping and close to transport, education and leisure activities, and is opposite Wellington Regional Hospital. The Wellington CBD is a 5 minute ride away.

Features include:

Dual key apartment - 1 bedroom + Studio
Built in 2013 to 150% of the current earthquake code standard
The complex is double glazed and fully insulated
Architecturally Designed by Novak & Middleton
Currently rented for a total of $905.00 p/w

These apartments are tightly held and command a great rent and a high occupancy.

Viewing is by appointment only.
