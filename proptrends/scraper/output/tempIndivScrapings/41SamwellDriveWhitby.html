Property details

Listing # WTB0091 | Listed on 2 August

Those with an appetite for compliance and a 'best left to the professionals belief' ! will truly appreciate this 4 bedroom, 2 bathroom, dual living space family home within leafy Samwell Drive, flanking the walkway network.

Re-clad with traditional real weatherboard (cavity system), new double glazed Aluminium window joinery, new kitchen & ensuite, and neutral decoration throughout provides the perfect, future proofed, turn-key opportunity to sit back and relax.

All the hard work is done.

Built favouring the street with double garaging and additional off-street parking, it means the generous rear section provides ample room for family outdoor living, complimented by expansive decking.

Don't delay, view now.
