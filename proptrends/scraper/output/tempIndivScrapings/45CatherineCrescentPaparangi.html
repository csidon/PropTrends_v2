Property details

Listing # JO29575 | Listed on 10 August

Perfectly preserved and waiting for new owners to make their own memories this three double bedroom home is the one you've been waiting for.

Situated a stones throw away from Paparangi Primary school and walking distance to Newlands College. On the bus route into the city centre so you can leave the car in the garage.

Open plan kitchen, dining and living with a traditional layout that has stood the test of time. New additions over the years of the heatpump and HRV system. The bathroom has also been recently renovated.

A great back yard with established bountiful fruit trees, a great spot for the BBQ or the trampoline. Low maintenance enough so you can enjoy your summer days.

We've got the builders report and LIM already available and think this one will be snapped up fast!
