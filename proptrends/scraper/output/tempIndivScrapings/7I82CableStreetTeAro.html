Property details

Listing # L22728400 | Listed on 25 July

Introducing an exceptional opportunity to own a remarkable three-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment in a prime location. Situated across the road from Waitangi Park, this waterfront property offers breathtaking views. Here are the key features:

  • Generous 96 sqm apartment with three bedrooms including storage
  • Well-appointed bathroom with modern fixtures
  • Captivating views and ample natural light
  • Seamless flow between spacious kitchen, dining, and living areas
  • Perfect for entertaining guests and enjoying family time
  • Convenient single garage for secure parking
  • BC rates of $10,881.75

This exceptional three-bedroom apartment presents a unique opportunity to own a prime piece of real estate in a sought-after location. With its waterfront access, stunning views, and close proximity to the New World supermarket, this property offers both convenience and luxury. The spacious open plan kitchen/dining and living area facing east creates a bright and welcoming atmosphere. Complete with a single garage for secure parking, this apartment is the perfect choice for those seeking a comfortable and stylish urban lifestyle. Don't miss out on this remarkable opportunity-schedule a viewing today!

Tender closes 12 noon Thursday 17th August 2023 at Tommy's Real Estate, 209 Victoria Street, Te Aro, Wellington (Vendor reserves the right to sell prior).
