Property details

Listing # JP2202 | Listed on 10 July

61 Onslow Road - blue-chip Khandallah real estate at its best. This high-end, expansive family home sits proudly on an elevated corner site, and boasts some of the most unrivalled, panoramic harbour and city views Wellington has to offer.

Quality, excellence and to maximise the view was the brief, and renowned architects Seddon Associates have certainly achieved this in spades. This super-sized executive level home benefits from lashings of all day sun, is immaculately presented, has privacy in abundance, and with its commanding street appeal, this is one seriously impressive family abode.

With its drive-on access, garaging for four, spa-pool, BBQ areas, low-maintenance gardens, and turfed playground for the kids, this is hosting central. Inside, no expense has been spared with quality fixtures and fittings installed throughout each level of the home. Floor vented gas central heating, underfloor heating in the bathrooms, an internal vac system, intercom entry, granite bench tops, dual basin vanities, separate child and adult entertainment rooms, and high-end appliances like a built-in Miele coffee machine for your enjoyment and comfort.

This superior four-bedroom executive family home is simply a must view.

  • A commanding 500m2 home on a 645m2 section (more or less)
  • Unrivalled and interrupted harbour and city views
  • Four light-filled double bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms; gas central heating
  • Three distinct elegant open-plan living areas; wall-mounted speakers
  • An entertainer's kitchen; separate adult and children's entertainment rooms
  • Easy drive on access; garaging for four; off-street parking for five+ vehicles
  • Low maintenance garden areas and impressive outdoor spaces

Sold by way of Negotiation, viewing by private appointment only. Price guide upwards of $2,995,000.

Don't let this fantastic opportunity to secure a seldom-found, highly desirable home pass you by. Contact Michael Hinds on 020 4144 3199 or Charles Morley-Hall on 021 666 600 for more information.
