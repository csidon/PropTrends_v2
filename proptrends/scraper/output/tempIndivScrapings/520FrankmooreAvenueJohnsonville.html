Property details

Listing # JO29545 | Listed on 10 August

Its hard to believe this private oasis is situated in the centre of Johnsonville. This two bedroom townhouse certainly ticks a lot of boxes, first home buyers, downsizers or investors!

Situated across the road from the community centre, library, café (with the best cheese scones and coffee in town!), pool, park and tennis club! You have the world at your doorstep, you can leave the car in one of the two off street parks and walk 5 minutes to the train station or bus stop into the city.

Split over two levels with two spacious bedrooms and bathroom upstairs then kitchen, open plan living and dining downstairs. The kitchen has been renovated and the garden transformed.

The vendors have got plans and are dedicated to getting their SOLD sign.

NO BODY CORPORATE FEES. Builders and LIM available - see you at the open home
