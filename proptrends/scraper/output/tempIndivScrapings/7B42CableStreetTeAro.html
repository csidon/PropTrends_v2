Property details

Listing # L23298000 | Listed on 1 August

BEO $1,295,000

Harbour Views - Sunny - Large - Modern - Central

Exceptional 3 bedroom waterfront apartment. And incredibly central. Directly across the street from Te Papa, and right next to Takina.

The living area boasts stunning inner-harbour views with direct access to wide north facing balcony. Smartly decorated and immaculately maintained. All on one level with ideal separation between the living area and the bedrooms. Big balcony windows guarantee the best of inner harbour views and loads of natural light.

This is superior apartment living with contemporary character to match.

PROPERTY VALUES:

Loads of room - 122 sqm floor area.

Three (3) large bedrooms. Extra large master bedroom has ensuite. Very generous open plan living / dining / kitchen area. Very smart and nicely appointed modern kitchen, bathroom and ensuite. Tastefully decorated with a modern twist, and spacious. Secure covered car park.

TALKING POINTS:

  • stunning Wellington harbour views
  • awesome neighbours - Te Papa and Takina
  • 7th floor elevation + outlook
  • wide open balcony
  • very well maintained
  • size and functionality
  • outstanding value (RV $1,475,000)
